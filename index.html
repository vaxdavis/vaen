About

Since 1984

Our passion is cars and our unique experience has brought us all together to share what we love. As a  group we have a collection that ranges from rare and vintage cars to some of the newest made, and we love to show them off. While we'll always appreciate the classics, we have expanded to include more modern day models, making way for the cars that are instant hits!

What We Look For

"I've never seen a car I didn't like" is our motto! All cars pre-1990 are welcome.  We pride ourselves with the diversity our club has to offer. Gear heads to novices are welcome!

Where To Find Us

Between all of our members, we have a diverse exciting collection. You can catch us all at a few local car nights as well as our monthly meet-ups at designated locations each month.
